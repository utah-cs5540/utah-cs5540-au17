The Overview

With the increasing amount of energy used today, people have found it difficult to keep track of their energy consumption. Many people forget to turn off what they don't use, and when they receive a high electricity bill they are not quite sure when or where the electricity is being used. Or how they can save more energy and money. The solution is NRGY, an energy consumption tracker to help people reduce energy usage by reminding them to turn off utilities and devices that are not being used, and by tracking driving habits and household utility consumption.

Features
