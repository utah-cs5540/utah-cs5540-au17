Study Smarter

Study Budy lets you get more out of your study time.

Find Out More

Studying is hard!

When preparing for standardized tests, students must study a large amount of material for a large number of hours in order to gain the necessary knowledge and skills to succeed. It is very difficult for them to get the most out of each study session. Study Buddy is designed to ensure that students can get the best study sessions possible.

See How!

Features

Test Taking Trainer

Improve your test taking skills with our Test Taking Trainer. Take a practice test while using Study Buddy, and the Test Taking Trainer will track your progress and give you tips on how you can improve your test taking skills.

P2P Tutoring

Use our P2P tutoring system to get on demand help from your peers by sharing your views and voice with each other.
